Delete Your HBox AI Account

Request the deletion of your HBox AI app account and all associated data.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Enter the username (or email) associated with your HBox AI app account below.
  2. Optionally provide a reason for your deletion request.
  3. Click "Submit Deletion Request" — this will open your email client with a pre-filled message to support@hbox.ai.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email within 2 business days.
  5. Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of confirmation.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Account profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Health vitals data (blood pressure, weight, glucose, pulse oximetry, temperature)
  • Care plans and medication reminders
  • Appointment history and virtual visit records
  • In-app messages and communication history
  • Connected device data and preferences

Data That May Be Retained

  • De-identified, aggregated analytics data (cannot be linked back to you)
  • Records required by law or regulatory compliance (e.g., HIPAA) — securely stored up to 7 years, then permanently deleted
  • Billing and transaction records per financial regulations — retained up to 3 years
