Delete Your HBox AI Account

Request the deletion of your HBox AI app account and all associated data.

Steps to Request Account Deletion
  1. Enter the username (or email address) associated with your HBox AI app account in the form below.
  2. Optionally, provide a reason for your deletion request.
  3. Click "Submit Deletion Request" to send your request to our support team at support@hbox.ai.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email within 2 business days acknowledging your request.
  5. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of confirmation.
Data That Will Be Deleted
  • Your account profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Health vitals data (blood pressure, weight, glucose readings, pulse oximetry, temperature)
  • Care plans and medication reminders
  • Appointment history and virtual visit records
  • In-app messages and communication history
  • Connected device data and preferences

Data That May Be Retained
  • De-identified and aggregated analytics data (cannot be linked back to you)
  • Records required to be retained by law or for regulatory compliance (e.g., HIPAA), which will be securely stored for up to 7 years as required and then permanently deleted
  • Billing and transaction records as required by applicable financial regulations, retained for up to 3 years
